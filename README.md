Aesthetic Considerations 

A website about South Africa should use the colours and images related to the country and its colourful flag. It cannot be too busy though, because it could become too bold and visually overwhelming for my users. 

I have tested my choice of only 3 colours on a desktop and mobile screen and my classmate John said that it was still easy to navigate (simple colours but good contrast) whilst still communicated South Africa because of my use of dark green and ochre which he associated with the Springboks.
