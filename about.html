About the L & R Dear Museum

Celebrating the proud history and traditions of Southland Boys' High School.

Our Story

The L & R Dear Museum is dedicated to preserving and celebrating the rich history of Southland Boys' High School. Through carefully preserved photographs, uniforms, trophies, archives, and memorabilia, the museum shares the stories of students, staff, and the wider school community who have helped shape the school's proud legacy. Every display reflects the values of excellence, leadership, service, and perseverance that have defined Southland Boys' High School for generations.

Visitors can explore significant milestones in the school's history, learn about outstanding sporting and academic achievements, and discover how the traditions of the school continue to inspire current and future students. Whether you are a current student, an Old Boy, a family member, or simply interested in Southland's educational heritage, the museum provides an engaging experience that connects the past with the present while preserving these stories for generations to come.

Our Mission

Our mission is to collect, preserve, and share the history of Southland Boys' High School while inspiring visitors to appreciate the achievements and traditions that continue to shape the school today.
