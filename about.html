About the L & R Dear Museum

The L & R Dear Museum celebrates the rich history, traditions, and achievements of Southland Boys' High School. Established to preserve the school's remarkable heritage, the museum showcases a diverse collection of photographs, trophies, uniforms, documents, and memorabilia that tell the story of generations of students, staff, and the wider school community. Every display highlights the values of excellence, leadership, service, and pride that have shaped Southland Boys' High School throughout its history. Visitors can explore significant milestones, discover inspiring stories of past students, and gain a deeper appreciation of the school's contribution to education, sport, culture, and the Southland community. Whether you are a current student, an old boy, a family member, or a first-time visitor, the museum offers a meaningful journey through the people, traditions, and events that continue to inspire future generations while preserving the legacy of those who came before.
